[Project Plan] Food Basket Optimization for Nigerian Households

This is a project management plan that adopts effective project management strategies to ensure the planning and execution of the project aimed at improving the quality and nutritional value of food baskets in Nigerian households. By providing an executive summary, stakeholder analysis, work breakdown structure, network diagram, activity-based budget, quality management plan, risk management plan, and lessons learned, this plan ensures the success of the project from start to finish.
