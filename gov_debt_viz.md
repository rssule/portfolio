The first visualization <i>(Viz 1)</i> is a bar chart showing general government debt in 2020 across 36 countries – alongside the OECD average and OECD total values. It is an ordered bar chart showing the ascending ranks (ordered list) of values from left to right. For this visualization, color is used to highlight the OECD average value by using balck for that bar and using grey for the other bars – creating an illusion of focus on the OECD average with the other bars in the background.
